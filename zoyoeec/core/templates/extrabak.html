Combine Postage

We do combine postage for multiple purchases. If you would like to purchase a few items, please do not do the check out until we send you an invoice with deducted postage.

Logistics Company

We use AUSPOST to send most of our items. We will update the track number once the item is out. If it is the peak season, AUSPOST may take a bit longer to process parcels. You can always track your item on http://auspost.com.au/track/track.html. We sometimes use Fastway to deliver our items as well. Delivery status can be checked from www.fastway.com.au.

Refund Policy

If an item is arrived broken, please contact us within 24 hours so that we can claim from AUSPOST or Fastway. If item is claimed after 24 hours, we are not responsible for the damage. In order to get refund, we also require customers to complete a PM26 form at local post office and send us an email with scanned PM26 form if item is broken or missing.
